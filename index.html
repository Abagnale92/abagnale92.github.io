About Me

I hold a master’s degree in game development, a master’s degree in computer science with a specialization in project management, and I have completed UI/UX and Game Design courses for video games at Digital Bros Academy. I also regularly take part in Game Jams, both for video games and board games.Thanks to my hybrid profile, I am able to follow a variety of professional activities and easily integrate into different creative and technical contexts.Currently, I work as a Technical Game Designer at SPICI in Naples, where I focus on advergames, gamification applications, and act as a consultant for the design of new ideas in the creative industry. The world of video games has always fascinated me, and over time I’ve developed a solid foundation in both game design and development. My goal is to gain a comprehensive understanding of the entire game development process—from both the design and technical sides-while leveraging my background in management to contribute across multiple areas, including QA and game production.
